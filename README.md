# 📊 Netflix Content Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Netflix content data to uncover insights about movies and TV shows. The dashboard visualizes trends across ratings, genres, countries, and release years to understand content distribution and growth patterns.

---

## 📖 Storyline

With the rapid growth of streaming platforms like Netflix, understanding content trends is essential for making data-driven decisions.

In this project, I explored the Netflix dataset to answer key questions such as:

* What type of content dominates Netflix — Movies or TV Shows?
* How has Netflix content evolved over time?
* Which countries contribute the most content?
* What kind of content is most popular among audiences?

By cleaning and analyzing the dataset, I transformed raw data into meaningful visual insights that highlight content patterns, audience preferences, and growth trends.

## 💡 Key Insights

* Movies dominate the Netflix catalog compared to TV Shows
* TV-MA is the most common rating, indicating a focus on mature content
* The United States produces the highest amount of content
* Content addition has increased significantly after 2015
